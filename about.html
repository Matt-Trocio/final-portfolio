More about me

I have always had a deep passion for performing, especially singing and dancing. From a young age, I found joy in expressing myself through music and movement, whether it was singing along to my favorite songs, learning new dance routines or making own. Over the years, I have honed my skills through practice, performances, and training with the support of my parents. Allowing me to grow as an confident performer. Whether on stage or in everyday life, singing and dancing have become an essential part of who I am, fueling my creativity and confidence.
